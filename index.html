KNOW YOUR FIGHTERS

Two AI mechs, two opposite philosophies. Read their styles, then bet on the one the matchup favors — the odds tell you who the model thinks will win, but not why. That part is up to you.

AXIOMPrecision Kiter

A glass cannon. Deadly accurate from range and hits like a truck, but fragile and slow to reload. Wins by keeping its distance and punishing every mistake. Loses if it gets cornered.

Best in: open, long-range modes
NEMESISAggressive Brawler

A pressure machine. Fast, tanky, and shreds at close range with a huge magazine. Wins by closing the gap and never letting up. Loses if it's kept at arm's length.

Best in: tight, close-quarters modes
HOW TO READ THE ODDS

The lower number (e.g. ×1.4) is the favorite — more likely to win, smaller payout. The higher number (e.g. ×3.0) is the underdog — less likely, bigger payout. Odds shift by mode: a close-quarters mode favors NEMESIS, a long-range one favors AXIOM. Bet the fighter whose style fits the arena.

⚡ Underdog bonus: backing the underdog pays extra — the bigger the longshot, the bigger the boost (look for the UNDERDOG +% tag on the bet button). High risk, high reward: you'll lose most of them, but an upset pays off big.

UPGRADES — one-time unlocks

Find these in the UPGRADES bar at the bottom-left, under the arena. Each is bought once with tokens, then toggled on/off for free. They're cosmetic or informational and never change who wins. On a touch screen, tap an upgrade once to see what it does and its price, then tap again to confirm.

TARGETING CALIBRATION

Lock the reticle inside the green zone. 5 shots — the tighter your timing, the more tokens you earn. Skill, not luck: a steady hand beats a lucky guess, but it'll never pay like a winning bet.
